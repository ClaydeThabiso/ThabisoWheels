The Evolution of Thabiso Wheels

Our journey from a local workshop to South Africa's premier customization destination has been fueled by the passion of our team and the trust of our clients.

We've cultivated a strong community on our social media platforms, where automotive enthusiasts celebrate our work and services. This digital presence has become a cornerstone of our growth, attracting new customers while allowing us to maintain relationships with our existing clients. Nothing brings us more satisfaction than seeing the smiles on our customers' faces when they experience our craftsmanship firsthand.

Accessibility remains one of our core values. Whether you prefer online consultations or in-person visits, we've designed our services to accommodate every preference. Currently operating from three strategic locations—Pretoria, Free State, and Cape Town—we're actively planning expansion across South Africa and beyond. For Thabiso Wheels, the sky is truly the limit.

"Customization isn't just about modifying vehicles—it's about creating moving art that reflects personality and passion."

Thabiso Shongwe, Founder & CEO

We invite you to join the conversation through our social platforms, email, or by visiting one of our branches. For those interested in learning more about our story, explore our digital magazine available in the sidebar.
