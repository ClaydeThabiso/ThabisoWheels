Pro Tips from the Garage

Ceramic Coating

The ultimate protection for your paint job that lasts 2-5 years with proper maintenance. Self-cleaning properties keep your car looking showroom fresh.

Performance Balance

Always match engine mods with upgraded brakes and suspension. A 500hp car with stock brakes is an accident waiting to happen.

Tire Investment

Premium tires can improve handling, braking, and acceleration more than any single performance mod. Don't cheap out where rubber meets road.

Lighting Personality

Custom RGB interior lighting controlled via smartphone adds personal flair to night drives. Choose colors that match your mood or music.
