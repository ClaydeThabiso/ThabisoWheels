OUR STORY

Transforming vehicles into masterpieces since 2021

From Township Workshop to National Leader

Founded in 2021 by Thabiso Shongwe with co-founders Khulekani Nyathikazi and Musa Lekhuleni, Thabiso Wheels began as a humble panelbeating shop in Ngondini township.

2021 - Humble Beginnings

Started with basic repairs and maintenance services, building our reputation one satisfied customer at a time.

2022 - The Turning Point

Recognized by Toyota and Ford for our exceptional craftsmanship, leading to strategic partnerships that propelled our growth.

Present - Industry Leaders

Now operating nationwide with a team of 50+ specialists, delivering award-winning customizations for discerning clients.

What began as a dream to make cars stand out has evolved into South Africa's premier customization destination. We combine technical expertise with artistic vision to create vehicles that turn heads and reflect their owners' personalities.
